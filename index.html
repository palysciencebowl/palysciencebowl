Paly Science Bowl

Our Mission

Paly Science Bowl is a student led organization that is committed to building passion in STEM by creating a tight-knit community of aspiring scientists and mathematicians who compete together. We compete in the National Science Bowl, a competition hosted by the United States Department of Energy.
