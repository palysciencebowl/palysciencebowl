About Science Bowl

  • -Fast paced Jeopardy-style buzzer competition

  • -Hosted by U.S. Department of Energy with tens of thousands of competitors nationwide

  • -Questions from 6 categories, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Math, Earth and Space Science, and Energy

  • -Questions are in multiple choice or short answer format
